A little about me...

Hi! My name is Sadie and I love creating things that live on the web and your mobile device! I first got started building things for the web in high school and turned it into a business in college. I loved being able to work with people and turn their visions into a reality. As time passed I discovered mobile apps (specifically games) and I fell in love. I wanted to build apps that people all around the world could enjoy.

Today, I have turned my passion for building and creating things from nothing to something into working as a Program Manager by day and a developer by night and I love it! I have worked with my dream software company Microsoft, at Boeing building amazing products, and at AppRiver, a cybersecurity company and each of them have truly shaped my career today. My main focus during the day is working with my internal and external customers and my development teams to build and maintain amazing products. Any other time I am creating, ideating and building apps for small and large businesses automating their existing processes, turning their dreams into realities for their customers and then working on a few games for myself! 🎮
